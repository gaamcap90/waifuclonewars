## HEX TILE ART (terrain re-theme to alien arena)

**CRITICAL RULES FOR ALL TILE PROMPTS:**
- Generate as **flat seamless texture only** — NO hex border, NO raised 3D edge, NO frame
- The game engine clips the image to hex shape automatically — just the flat surface content
- Style: **top-down isometric view**, illustrated/painted, matching the existing pixel-art tile aesthetic
- Size: 512×512 px, square image (game clips it to hex)
- Think: "what would you see if you looked straight down at this terrain from above"

## TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

1. **Midjourney**: Add `--no text, watermark, signature` to all prompts
2. **Leonardo.ai**: Use **Absolute Reality** or **DreamShaper** model for miniature look
3. **Meshy.ai** (free): Upload any portrait and click "Image to 3D" for GLB model export  
4. **Tripo.ai** (free): Best for character figures — feed the Midjourney output
5. **Background removal**: Use **remove.bg** (free tier) to get transparent PNGs
6. **Upscaling**: Use **Magnific AI** or **Real-ESRGAN** for 2× upscale before import
